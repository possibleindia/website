ABOUT US

POSSIBLE INDIA was established on 4-11-2013. POSSIBLE INDIA is a NGO which is a voluntary organization for achieving specific purpose,service being the main moto and the mission. POSSIBLE INDIA is helping orphans providing Education and aiming towards AIDS affected children. Their outreach plans include home health care ,hospice care,woman empowerment , providing awareness to people by making short movies and street plays. POSSIBLE INDIA also aims for outdoor recreational activities in a safe , clean and inviting environment for people by giving them awareness.
