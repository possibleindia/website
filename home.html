POSSIBLE INDIA


POSSIBLE INDIA was established on 4th November 2013. POSSIBLE INDIA is a NGO which is a voluntary
organization with a purpose of serving human being.
POSSIBLE INDIA is helping orphans providing education and aiming towards
AIDS affected children. Their outreach plans include home health care, hospice care, woman empowerment,
providing awareness to people by making short movies and street plays.
POSSIBLE INDIA also aims for outdoor recreational activities in a safe,
clean and inviting environment for people by giving them awareness.
