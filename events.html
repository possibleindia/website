Events

Inaugration

POSSIBLE INDIA was inaugurated on 4-11- 2013 , which was inaugurated by many of great personalities like teachers , social workers and politicians too. On the day of inauguration many numbers of plants were planted and distributed too. On the day of inauguration the members of this team took a pledge to see better progressing India and conducting welfareness programs among people.

Rally

POSSIBLE INDA conducted a rally on 1/12/2013 for spreading awareness on AIDS. On this day many school kids took part in this rally with the help of teachers and by the support of many politicians . This rally was about 7 kms and a small amount of money was collected from the shopkeepers and some amount from the team members and it was denoted to AIDS affected people and children

Street Play

A street play was done on 1/4/2017 . This street play was about women empowerment , women education and fight against dowry . This street play was acted by members of POSSIBLE INDIA . A kannada slogan was recited “HENNU ABALE ALLA , SABALE” which means Women is capable of doing any wonder in the world.
